CHAPTER ______

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Baltimore City – Natural Resources – Archery
3 Hunting Safety Zones

4 For the purpose of adding
5 Baltimore City to the list of jurisdictions in which the safety zone for archery hunters
6 extends a certain distance from certain structures; and generally relating to hunting
7 in Baltimore City.

8 BY repealing and reenacting, without amendments,
9 Article – Natural Resources
10 Section 10–410(g)(1)
11 Annotated Code of Maryland
12 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

13 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,
14 Article - Natural Resources
15 Section 10-410(g)(3)(i)
16 Annotated Code of Maryland
17 (2023 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

18 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
19 That the Laws of Maryland read as follows:

20 Article – Natural Resources

21 10–410.

22 (g) (1) Except as provided in paragraphs (2) and (3) of this subsection, a
23 person, other than the owner or occupant, while hunting for any wild bird or mammal may
24 not shoot or discharge any firearm or other deadly weapon within 150 yards, known as the
25 “safety zone”, of a dwelling house, residence, church, or other building or camp occupied by
26 human beings, or shoot at any wild bird or mammal while it is within this area, without
27 the specific advance permission of the owner or occupant.

28 (3) (i) For archery hunters in Allegany County, BALTIMORE CITY,
29 Calvert County, Carroll County, Cecil County, Frederick County, Garrett County, Harford
30 County, Montgomery County, St. Mary’s County, Washington County, Worcester County,
31 or Wicomico County, the safety zone described in paragraph (1) of this subsection extends
32 for 50 yards from a dwelling house, residence, church, or any other building or camp
33 occupied by human beings.

34 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
35 1, 2026.
