2 SENATE BILL 559

1 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,

2 (a) In this section, “Program” means the Recreational Fishery Data Collection
3 Program.

4 (b) On or before December 31, 2026, the Department of Natural Resources shall
5 establish the Recreational Fishery Data Collection Program.

6 (c) The purpose of the Program is to develop and implement a data collection
7 system for recreational anglers in the State.

8 (d) In coordination with State and federal partners, the Department of Natural
9 Resources shall:

10 (1) survey and collect data on recreational fishing reporting programs in
11 other states; and

12 (2) subject to available funding, enter into a contract for the development
13 and implementation of a data collection system for recreational anglers.

14 (e) On or before December 31, 2027, the Department of Natural Resources shall
15 report to the Senate Committee on Education, Energy, and the Environment and the House
16 Environment and Transportation Committee, in accordance with § 2–1257 of the State
17 Government Article, on the findings of the Program, including a workplan detailing the
18 proposed development and implementation of a data collection system for recreational
19 anglers.

20 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
21 ~~January~~ July 1, ~~2027.~~ 2026. It shall remain effective for a period of 1 year and 6 months
22 and, at the end of December 31, 2027, this Act, with no further action required by the
23 General Assembly, shall be abrogated and of no further force and effect.
