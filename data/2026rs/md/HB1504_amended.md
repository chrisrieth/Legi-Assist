1 AN ACT concerning

2 Highways – Sidewalks and Bicycle Pathways – Construction and
3 Reconstruction
4 (The Honorable Anne Healey Pedestrian Safety Act of 2026)

5 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Highway Administration to prioritize certain
6 funding for the construction and reconstruction of sidewalks and bicycle pathways
7 to sidewalks and bicycle pathways that are, or are adjacent to highways that are,
8 subject to a complete streets policy or another similar Vision Zero program; requiring
9 a local government to request certain funding from the Department of
10 Transportation in a certain manner; authorizing certain State funding for the
11 construction or reconstruction of sidewalks or bicycle pathways under certain
12 circumstances; and generally relating to the construction and reconstruction of
13 sidewalks and bicycle pathways.

3 (a) (1) In this section the following words have the meanings indicated.

4 (2) “Municipal corporation” means a municipality as defined in § 1–101 of
5 the Local Government Article.

6 (3) “Urban highway” means a highway, other than an expressway, that is:

7 (i) 1. Constructed with a curb and gutter and an enclosed type
8 storm drainage system;

9 2. Located in an urban area and on which is located a public
10 facility that creates appreciable pedestrian traffic along the highway from adjacent areas;

11 3. Located within urban boundaries as defined by the U.S.
12 Census Bureau; or

13 4. Located within the boundaries of a municipal corporation;
14 and

15 (ii) Part of the State highway system.

16 (b) (1) Sidewalks shall be constructed at the time of construction or
17 reconstruction of an urban highway, or in response to the request of a local government
18 unless:

19 (i) The Administration determines that the cost or impacts of
20 constructing the sidewalks would be too great in relation to the need for them or their
21 probable use; or

22 (ii) The local government indicates that there is no need for
23 sidewalks.

24 (2) Sidewalks constructed under this section shall be consistent with area
25 master plans and transportation plans adopted by the local planning commission.

26 (c) (1) If sidewalks or bicycle pathways are constructed or reconstructed as
27 part of a roadway construction or reconstruction project, the Administration shall fund the
28 sidewalk or bicycle pathway construction or reconstruction as a part of the cost of the
29 roadway project.

31 sidewalks or bicycle pathways are constructed or reconstructed in response to a request
