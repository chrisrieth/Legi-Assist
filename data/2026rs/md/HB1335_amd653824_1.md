AMENDMENT NO. 1
On page 1, strike beginning with “Recruitment,” in line 2 down through
“Professionals” in line 3 and substitute “Information Technology Staffing and
Capability Assessment”; and in line 7, strike “recruitment, retention, and
compensation” and substitute “staffing, capability assessment, and workforce
alignment”.

(ii) an evaluation of each principal department’s information
technology environment, including:

1. the size and information technology needs of the
principal department;

2. the size and scope of systems and applications used by
