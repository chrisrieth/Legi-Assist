18 SUBTITLE 6B. WORKER RESIDENCY REQUIREMENTS.

19 17–6B–01.

20 (A) IN THIS SUBTITLE THE FOLLOWING WORDS HAVE THE MEANINGS
21 INDICATED.

22 (B) “APPRENTICESHIP HOUR” MEANS 1 HOUR OF TIME SPENT IN AN
23 APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAM AND MAY INCLUDE OBSERVATION, PERFORMING WORK,
24 OR WORK–RELATED DISCUSSION.

25 (C) “COVERED LARGE PROJECT” MEANS A PUBLIC WORK CONTRACT
26 SUBJECT TO SUBTITLE 2 OF THIS TITLE THAT IS VALUED AT $5,000,000 OR MORE.

27 (D) “DEPARTMENT” MEANS THE MARYLAND DEPARTMENT OF LABOR.

28 (E) “REGISTERED APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAM” MEANS AN
29 APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAM REGISTERED WITH THE DIVISION OF WORKFORCE
30 DEVELOPMENT AND ADULT LEARNING WITHIN THE DEPARTMENT.

2 17–6B–02.

3 (A) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SUBSECTION (B) OF THIS SECTION, THIS
4 SUBTITLE APPLIES ONLY TO:

13 (VI) PRINCE GEORGE’S COUNTY.

14 (B) THIS SUBTITLE DOES NOT APPLY TO A STATE CONTRACT FOR WHICH
15 THE FEDERAL GOVERNMENT PROVIDES MONEY.

16 17–6B–03.

17 (A) A CONTRACTOR THAT IS AWARDED A PROCUREMENT CONTRACT FOR A
18 COVERED LARGE PROJECT SHALL ENSURE THAT, FOR THE APPLICABLE PROJECT,
19 AT LEAST 35% OF APPRENTICESHIP HOURS ARE PERFORMED BY STATE RESIDENTS.

20 (B) AS A CONDITION OF RECEIVING THE CONTRACT, A CONTRACTOR
21 SUBJECT TO THIS SUBTITLE SHALL PROVIDE TO THE UNIT AND THE DEPARTMENT A
22 STATEMENT ATTESTING THAT THE CONTRACTOR SHALL MEET THE REQUIREMENTS
23 OF THIS SECTION FOR THE APPLICABLE PROJECT.

24 17–6B–04.

25 (A) THE SECRETARY MAY ADOPT REGULATIONS AUTHORIZING A
26 CONTRACTOR, AS AN ALTERNATIVE TO FULFILLING THE REQUIREMENTS OF §
27 17–6B–03 OF THIS SUBTITLE, TO ELECT TO MAKE A CONTRIBUTION TO:

1 (1) THE STATE APPRENTICESHIP TRAINING FUND ESTABLISHED

3 (2) A REGISTERED APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAM OPERATING AND

7 (C) WHEN DETERMINING COMPLIANCE WITH THIS SUBTITLE, APPLICABLE
8 PERCENTAGES ESTABLISHED UNDER § 17–6B–03 OF THIS SUBTITLE SHALL BE
9 CALCULATED BY ROUNDING TO THE NEAREST WHOLE NUMBER.

10 17–6B–05.

11 A CONTRACTOR THAT EXCEEDS THE REQUIREMENTS OF § 17–6B–03(A) OF
12 THIS SUBTITLE ON A CONTRACT MAY APPLY THE EXCESS APPRENTICESHIP HOURS
13 TO ANOTHER CONTRACT SUBJECT TO THIS SUBTITLE THAT IS ENTERED INTO BY THE
14 CONTRACTOR WITHIN 2 YEARS AFTER THE COMPLETION OF THE FIRST CONTRACT.

15 17–6B–06.

16 (A) A CONTRACTOR THAT FAILS TO MEET THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS
17 SUBTITLE SHALL BE LIABLE FOR AN AMOUNT EQUAL TO TWICE THE NUMBER OF
18 APPRENTICESHIP HOURS AT THE PAYMENT RATE SET UNDER § 17–605 OF THIS TITLE.

19 (B) PAYMENTS COLLECTED UNDER THIS SECTION SHALL BE PAID TO:

20 (1) THE STATE APPRENTICESHIP TRAINING FUND ESTABLISHED
21 UNDER § 17–602 OF THIS TITLE; OR

22 (2) A REGISTERED APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAM OPERATING AND
23 TRAINING APPRENTICES IN THE STATE OR AN ORGANIZATION THAT HAS
24 REGISTERED APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAMS LOCATED IN THE STATE, FOR THE
25 PURPOSE OF SUPPORTING THESE PROGRAMS.

26 (C) THE SECRETARY SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS TO ESTABLISH
27 ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURES FOR THE COLLECTION AND DISTRIBUTION OF
28 PAYMENTS UNDER THIS SECTION.
