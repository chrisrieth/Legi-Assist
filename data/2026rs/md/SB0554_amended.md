1 (m) (1) The student member shall be a student regularly enrolled in the
2 student’s junior or senior year of high school in the Baltimore City Public School System
3 who shall be elected by the high school students of the Baltimore City Public School System,
4 in accordance with procedures established by the board in collaboration with the Associated
5 Student Congress of Baltimore City.

6 (2) The term of a student member is 1 year.

7 (3) A student member may not serve more than two consecutive full terms.

8 (4) The student member may vote on matters before the board, including
9 those relating to:

10 (i) Capital and operating budgets;

11 (ii) School closings, reopenings, and boundaries; and

12 (iii) Student disciplinary matters.

13 (5) The student member may not vote on matters before the board relating
14 to personnel and collective bargaining decisions.

15 (6) The student member may attend or participate in an executive or
16 special session of the board.

17 (n) Any action by the board shall require:

18 (1) A quorum of a majority of the voting members then serving; and
