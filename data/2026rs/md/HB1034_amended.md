A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Child Care – Child Abuse and Neglect – Training

3 Requirements

4 FOR the purpose of requiring child care providers in registered family child care
5 homes and registered large family child care homes and licensees and staff of a child
6 care center to complete certain training on the detection and reporting of child abuse
7 at certain intervals; and generally relating to child abuse and neglect.
