AMENDMENTS TO HOUSE BILL 1526
(First Reading File Bill)

AMENDMENT NO. 1
On page 1, in line 7, after “sell” insert “, prepare, and serve”; and in line 8, after
“consumption;” insert “authorizing the holder of the permit to use the licensed premises
for events;”.

AMENDMENT NO. 2
On page 4, in line 6, after “(D)” insert “(1)”; in lines 6 and 13, in each instance,
after “SELL” insert “, PREPARE, AND SERVE”; in lines 7, 8, 9, and 13, strike “(1)”, “(2)”,
“(3)”, and “(E)”, respectively, and substitute “(I)”, “(II)”, “(III)”, and “(2)”, respectively;
in line 14, strike “SECTION” and substitute “SUBSECTION”; and after line 15, insert:

“(E) (1) THE PERMIT AUTHORIZES THE HOLDER TO USE THE LICENSED
PREMISES FOR EVENTS.

(2) ANY ON–PREMISES CONSUMPTION OF ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES
AT AN EVENT HELD IN ACCORDANCE WITH PARAGRAPH (1) OF THIS SUBSECTION
SHALL COMPLY WITH SUBSECTION (D) OF THIS SECTION.”.
