A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Emergency Room Services and Post–Acute Care – Coverage and Facility Studies

3 For the purpose of authorizing the Maryland Insurance Commissioner to conduct an
4 examination of certain decisions by carriers related to claims or authorization
5 requests for services in, or related to services in, emergency departments;
6 authorizing the Commissioner to have certain decisions independently reviewed
7 under certain circumstances; requiring that a certain report required to be compiled
8 by the Commissioner include data on certain adverse decisions and grievances;
9 requiring the Maryland Health Care Commission, in conjunction with the Health
10 Services Cost Review Commission, to conduct a study to quantify bed capacity in
11 hospitals and post–acute settings in the State and make a recommendation on a
12 certain collection and auditing process; requiring the Maryland Health Care
13 Commission, in consultation with the Health Services Cost Review Commission, to
14 study analyzing options to facilitate clinically appropriate transitions from acute to
15 post–acute care settings in the State; and generally relating to emergency room
16 services and post–acute care.
