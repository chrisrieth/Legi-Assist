Horse Racing - Laurel Park - Arabian Breed Racing Authorization 
 
 
This emergency bill authorizes a licensee at Laurel Park to conduct live racing of  
Arabian breed horses under specified conditions. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  None. The bill does not directly affect State operations or finances. 
  
Local Effect:  None. 
 
Small Business Effect:  None. 
 
 
Analysis 
 
Current Law:  A licensee at Pimlico Race Course may conduct live racing of  
Arabian breed horses under specified conditions, including the approval of the State Racing 
Commission. 
 
An Arabian breed horse is a horse that (1) is a purebred Arabian breed horse and (2) has a 
valid certificate of registry with the Arabian Jockey Club of America. 
 
 
Additional Information 
 
Recent Prior Introductions:  Similar legislation has not been introduced within the last 
three years. 
 
Designated Cross File:  SB 453 (Senator Jennings) - Budget and Taxation.
