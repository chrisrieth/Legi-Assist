A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Baltimore County – Alcoholic Beverages Licenses – Transfers and Conversions

3 FOR the purpose of requiring certain Class B beer, wine, and liquor licenses in existence on
4 a certain date to be converted into certain Class B hotel and restaurant licenses before
5 a certain date; repealing certain prohibitions on a transfer to different premises or a
6 conversion to a different class of license that apply to certain alcoholic beverages
7 licenses issued, reissued, or transferred by the Board of License Commissioners for
8 Baltimore County; and generally relating to alcoholic beverages licenses in
9 Baltimore County.

231 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, notwithstanding any
232 license population quota limitation established by the Board of License Commissioners
233 for Baltimore County, the following Class B beer, wine, and liquor licenses in existence
234 on July 1, 2026, shall be converted into Class B hotel and restaurant licenses under §
235 13-902 of the Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis Article:

236 (1) Class B (Towson Commercial Revitalization District) licenses under
237 § 13-1705 of the Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis Article;

238 (2) Class B (HV) restaurant (on–sale) licenses under § 13-1706 of the
239 Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis Article;

240 (3) Class B (QG), (MCOM), or (PC) restaurant (on–sale) licenses under
241 § 13-1707 of the Alcoholic Beverages and Cannabis Article;

242 (4) Class B (MSF) (on–sale) licenses under § 13-1709 of the Alcoholic
243 Beverages and Cannabis Article;

244 (5) Class B (OMTC) (on–sale) licenses established under Chapter 423 of
245 the Acts of the General Assembly of 1996; and

246 (6) Class B (TRD) restaurant (on–sale) licenses established under
247 Chapter 365 of the Acts of the General Assembly of 2003.
