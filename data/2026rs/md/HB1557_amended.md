A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Procurement – Real Estate Advisory Committee and Property Acquisition and
3 Lease Requirements

4 FOR the purpose of establishing a Real Estate Advisory Committee in the Department of
5 General Services to advise the Department, the Board of Public Works, and the General
6 Assembly on certain real estate matters; establishing additional requirements for the
7 Board of Public Works before approving the acquisition or lease of certain real
8 property; and generally relating to real estate transactions in State government.
