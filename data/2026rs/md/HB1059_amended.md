CHAPTER ______

1 AN ACT concerning

2 State Department of Education – Asian American History – Accurate Instruction

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Department of Education to establish a program
4 designed to assist with the accurate teaching of Asian American history; requiring the
5 Department to identify certain courses and materials and make available a certain
6 professional development course; and generally relating to a program assisting with the
7 accurate teaching of Asian American history.

8 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,
9 That:

10 (a) The State Department of Education shall establish a program designed to
11 assist with the accurate teaching of Asian American history in accordance with this
12 section.

13 (b) (1) The Department shall identify existing courses and materials that can
14 be adapted into a professional development course on Asian American history for educators
15 in the State from the following entities:

16 (i) the Smithsonian Institution;

17 (ii) the Asian American Education Project;

18 (iii) Teach AAPI;

19 (iv) the Asian Pacific Islander Desi American Resource Center at San
20 Diego State University;

21 (v) the Sikh Coalition;

22 (vi) the National Council for Social Studies; and

23 (vii) the Asian American Foundation.

1 (2) Based on the material the Department is able to collect from the entities
2 described under paragraph (1) of this subsection, and using existing resources, the
3 Department shall make available a professional development course on Asian American
4 history by:

5 (i) developing a course;

6 (ii) adapting an existing course developed by an entity listed under
7 paragraph (1) of this subsection; or

8 (iii) making available an existing course offered by an entity listed
9 under paragraph (1) of this subsection.

10 (3) A professional development course offered in accordance with this
11 subsection shall be aligned with the State Board of Education’s existing social studies
12 standards.

13 (c) In the 2026–2027 and 2027–2028 school years, the Department shall identify
14 age–appropriate materials for use in instruction during Asian American history month and
15 disseminate the materials to each county board of education.

16 (d) In carrying out its duties under this section, the Department shall consult
17 with academics, organizations, and leaders with expertise in Asian American history.

18 (e) (1) On or before December 1, 2028, the State Department of Education
19 shall report on the results of its activities under this section, in accordance with § 2–1257
20 of the State Government Article, to the General Assembly.

21 (2) The Department shall include in its report under this subsection,
22 information about Asian American history that, in school years 2026–2027 and 2027–2028,
23 has been incorporated into the program of instruction in public schools in the State.

24 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect July
25 1, 2026. It shall remain effective for a period of 3 years and, at the end of June 30, 2029,
26 this Act, with no further action required by the General Assembly, shall be abrogated and
27 of no further force and effect.
