A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Health, Health Insurance, and Health Occupations – Perinatal Behavioral Health
3 Conditions

4 FOR the provide a standing referral to a behavioral health care
5 provider for a certain period of time; requiring the Maryland Medical Assistance
6 Program and certain insurers, nonprofit health service plans, and health
7 maintenance organizations to provide coverage for screening for perinatal behavioral
8 health conditions at certain times; altering and establishing requirements related to
9 the identification and development of training programs that improve early
10 identification of perinatal behavioral health conditions; requiring certain health care
11 providers to screen for perinatal behavioral health conditions; requiring the Maryland
12 Department of Health to identify certain screening tools and to assist certain health
13 care providers with accessing resources and referral services related to screening for
14 perinatal behavioral health conditions; requiring health occupations boards to grant a
15 certain number of hours of continuing education credits for each hour of continuing
16 education completed by certain individuals on perinatal behavioral health conditions;
17 and generally relating to perinatal behavioral health conditions.
