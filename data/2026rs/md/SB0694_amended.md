1 (a) Any person claiming to be aggrieved by an alleged discriminatory act may file

3 (c) (1) (i) Except as provided in paragraphs (2) and (3) of this subsection, a

6 (ii) A complaint filed with a federal or local human relations

11 discriminatory act occurred.

14 to have complied with subparagraph (i) of this paragraph.

20 subparagraph (i) of this paragraph.
