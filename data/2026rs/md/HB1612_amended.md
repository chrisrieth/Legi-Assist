CHAPTER ______

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Gaming – Campaign Financing Contributions and Study on
3 Independent Evaluation of Sports Wagering

4 repealing the prohibition on applicants for and holders of video lottery operation licenses and
5 persons who own an interest in video lottery facility operations in the State from
6 directly or indirectly making contributions to certain campaign finance entities;
7 requiring the State Lottery and Gaming Control Agency to study and report to the
8 Governor and the General Assembly, on or before a certain date, on certain matters
9 concerning certain independent evaluators for sports wagering
10 content; and generally relating to gaming.
