A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Natural Resources – Recreational Fishery Data Collection Program – Established

3 FOR the purpose of establishing the Recreational Fishery Data Collection Program in the
4 Department of Natural Resources to develop and implement a data collection system for
5 recreational anglers in the State; and generally relating to the Recreational Fishery Data
6 Collection Program.

21 SECTION 1. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF MARYLAND,

(a) In this section, “Program” means the Recreational Fishery Data Collection
Program.

(b) On or before December 31, 2026, the Department of Natural Resources
shall establish the Recreational Fishery Data Collection Program.

(c) The purpose of the Program is to develop and implement a data collection
system for recreational anglers in the State.

(d) In coordination with State and federal partners, the Department of
Natural Resources shall:

(1) survey and collect data on recreational fishing reporting programs
in other states; and

(2) subject to available funding, enter into a contract for the
development and implementation of a data collection system for recreational anglers.

(e) On or before December 31, 2027, the Department of Natural Resources
shall report to the Senate Committee on Education, Energy, and the Environment and
the House Environment and Transportation Committee, in accordance with § 2–1257 of
the State Government Article, on the findings of the Program, including a workplan
detailing the proposed development and implementation of a data collection system for
recreational anglers.

21 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
22 July 1, 2026. It shall remain effective for a period of 1 year and 6 months and, at the end of December 31, 2027, this Act, with no further action required by the General Assembly, shall be abrogated and of no further force and effect.
