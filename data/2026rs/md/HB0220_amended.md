A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Environment – Water – Individual Submeters

3 FOR the purpose of authorizing the installation of individual submeters for certain
4 apartment houses and mobile home parks; prohibiting the owner, operator, or manager
5 of an apartment house or a mobile home park, or a contractor hired by the owner, operator, or manager,
6 from imposing certain costs on a unit; authorizing the owner, operator, or manager of an apartment
7 house or a mobile home park to bill an occupant a certain amount under certain
8 circumstances; prohibiting the owner, operator, or manager of an apartment house or a
9 mobile home park from billing an occupant under certain circumstances; requiring the owner,
10 operator, or manager of an apartment house or a mobile home park, or a contractor hired by the owner,
11 operator, or manager to maintain certain records; prohibiting unpaid water bills
12 from being treated as unpaid rent in an eviction process; establishing a certain
13 complaint process; requiring certain landlords to use a certain written lease,
14 provide a copy of a water or sewer bill to a tenant within a certain time frame, and notify
15 prospective tenants of certain information within a certain time frame; prohibiting
16 certain landlords from requiring a tenant to make certain payments to a third party; and generally relating to individual submeters.

24 (I) THE TOTAL WATER AND SEWER COSTS BILLED TO THE
25 OCCUPANTS OF THE UNIT IN THE IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING 2 YEARS; OR

26 (II) IF WATER AND SEWER COSTS WERE NOT BILLED TO AN
27 OCCUPANT OF THE UNIT FOR THE IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING 2 YEARS, THE
28 TOTAL WATER AND SEWER COSTS BILLED TO A COMPARABLY SIZED UNIT WITHIN
29 THAT TIME PERIOD.

30 (C) NOTWITHSTANDING ANY OTHER PROVISION OF LAW, A LANDLORD
31 THAT DOES NOT MEET THE REQUIREMENTS OF § 9–1115 OF THE ENVIRONMENT
32 ARTICLE MAY NOT REQUIRE A TENANT TO MAKE PAYMENTS TO A THIRD PARTY
