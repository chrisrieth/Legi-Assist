A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 County Boards of Education – Student Electronic Communication Device Use
3 Policy – Establishment
4 (Maryland Phone–Free Schools Act)

5 FOR the purpose of requiring each county board of education to develop and implement,
6 not later than a certain school year, a policy prohibiting the use of an electronic
7 communication device by a student during the school day except under certain
8 circumstances; and generally relating to county boards of education and a student
9 electronic communication device use policy.

10 BY adding to
11 Article – Education
12 Section 7–137
13 Annotated Code of Maryland
14 (2025 Replacement Volume and 2025 Supplement)

23 BELL RINGS AT THE START OF THE SCHOOL DAY UNTIL THE DISMISSAL BELL RINGS

3 (3) (I) “ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE” MEANS ANY
4 PORTABLE ELECTRONIC DEVICE CARRIED OR TRANSPORTED BY A STUDENT TO
5 RECEIVE, SEND, OR RECORD VOICE, IMAGE, OR TEXT CONTENT, INCLUDING CELL
6 PHONES, TABLETS, LAPTOPS, SMART WATCHES, WIRELESS EARBUDS OR
7 HEADPHONES, GEOLOCATION DEVICES, AND ANY OTHER DEVICE WITH
8 COMMUNICATION FUNCTIONS.

9 (II) “ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE” DOES NOT
10 INCLUDE AN ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE PROVIDED BY A COUNTY BOARD
11 FOR INSTRUCTIONAL PURPOSES.

12 (4) “SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM” MEANS AN INTERNET–BASED
13 APPLICATION, WEBSITE, OR DIGITAL SERVICE THAT ENABLES USERS TO CREATE,
14 SHARE, OR VIEW CONTENT OR TO INTERACT WITH OTHER USERS THROUGH
15 PROFILES, ACCOUNTS, FEEDS, MESSAGING, COMMENTS, REACTIONS, OR SIMILAR
16 SOCIAL FEATURES, AND THAT IS PRIMARILY DESIGNED TO FACILITATE SOCIAL
17 INTERACTION, USER–GENERATED CONTENT, OR CONTENT DISTRIBUTION TO A
18 BROAD OR SEMI–PUBLIC AUDIENCE.

19 (5) “STORE” MEANS TO SECURE IN A MANNER DETERMINED BY THE
20 COUNTY BOARD.

21 (B) NOT LATER THAN THE 2027–2028 SCHOOL YEAR, EACH COUNTY BOARD
22 SHALL DEVELOP AND IMPLEMENT A POLICY TO PROHIBIT THE USE OF ELECTRONIC
23 COMMUNICATION DEVICES BY STUDENTS DURING THE ACADEMIC SCHOOL DAY.

24 (C) EXCEPT AS PROVIDED IN SUBSECTION (D) OF THIS SECTION, THE
25 POLICY DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION SHALL:

26 (1) PROHIBIT A STUDENT FROM USING AN ELECTRONIC
27 COMMUNICATION DEVICE DURING THE ACADEMIC SCHOOL DAY;

28 (2) REQUIRE A STUDENT TO STORE THE STUDENT’S ELECTRONIC
29 COMMUNICATION DEVICE DURING THE ACADEMIC SCHOOL DAY;

30 (3) PROHIBIT A STUDENT FROM ACCESSING OR USING A SOCIAL
31 MEDIA PLATFORM DURING THE ACADEMIC SCHOOL DAY; AND

32 (4) REQUIRE ADMINISTRATOR APPROVAL FOR THE USE OF A
33 PERSONAL ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE FOR EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES
34 WHEN A SCHOOL–ISSUED ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE IS UNAVAILABLE.

35 (D) THE POLICY DEVELOPED UNDER THIS SECTION MAY NOT PROHIBIT A
36 STUDENT FROM USING AN ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATION DEVICE:
