CHAPTER ______

1 AN ACT concerning

2 Metro Funding Modification Act of 2026

3 FOR the purpose of requiring the Governor to withhold a certain percentage of the annual
4 appropriation for grants to the Washington Suburban Transit District under certain
5 circumstances; altering the calculation of the amount that the Governor is required
6 to include in the annual State budget each year to provide grants to the Washington
7 Suburban Transit District to pay the capital costs of the Washington Metropolitan
8 Area Transit Authority; requiring the Governor to include an appropriation in the
9 annual State budget bill for the purpose of paying the State’s proportionate share of
10 funding required under a certain agreement; requiring the Department to withhold
11 certain grants for any fiscal year in which the Authority invests in a rail signaling
12 system without reaching certain collective bargaining agreements; making this Act
13 contingent on enactment of certain legislation by the Commonwealth of Virgina and the
14 District of Columbia; and generally relating to funding for the Washington
15 Metropolitan Area Transit Authority.

16 BY repealing and reenacting, with amendments,

12 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That:

13 (a) Section 1 of this Act is contingent on:

14 (1) the Commonwealth of Virginia enacting legislation that provides
15 dedicated capital funding for the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority that
16 includes:

17 (i) a mandatory appropriation for the Commonwealth of Virginia’s
18 proportionate share of the dedicated funding required by the Capital Funding Agreement
19 Subsidy Allocation Formula, or any successor agreement; and

20 (ii) an annual 3% increase to the mandatory appropriation; and

21 (2) the District of Columbia enacting legislation that provides dedicated
22 capital funding for the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority that includes:

23 (i) a mandatory appropriation for the District of Columbia’s
24 proportionate share of the dedicated funding required by the Capital Funding Agreement
25 Subsidy Allocation Formula, or any successor agreement; and

26 (ii) an annual 3% increase to the base amount of capital funding.

27 (b) The Department of Transportation shall notify the Department of Legislative
28 Services in writing within 5 days after both the Commonwealth of Virginia and the District
29 of Columbia have enacted legislation that meets the requirements of subsection (a) of this
30 section.

31 (c) Section 1 of this Act shall take effect on the date that the Department of
32 Legislative Services receives notice under subsection (b) of this section.

33 SECTION 3. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That, subject to Section 2 of this
