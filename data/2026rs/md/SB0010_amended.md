A BILL ENTITLED

1 AN ACT concerning

2 State Highway Administration – Speed Monitoring Systems – Application
3 Approval

4 FOR the purpose of requiring the State Highway Administration to approve, deny, or
5 return as incomplete an application for the use of a certain speed monitoring system
6 within a certain number of days; providing that if the Administration does not
7 approve, deny, or return the application in accordance with this Act, the application
8 is deemed approved; limiting the number of applications that may be submitted
9 within a certain period of time; and generally relating to applications for the use of
10 speed monitoring systems.

1 (9) “Speed monitoring system” means a device with one or more motor
2 vehicle sensors producing recorded images of motor vehicles traveling at speeds at least 12
3 miles per hour above the posted speed limit.

4 (b) (1) (ii) 1. Before a county may use a speed monitoring system on a
5 State highway at a location within a municipal corporation, the county shall:

6 [1.] A. Obtain the approval of the State Highway
7 Administration;

8 [2.] B. Notify the municipal corporation of the State
9 Highway Administration’s approval of the use of a speed monitoring system at that
10 location; and

11 [3.] C. Grant the municipal corporation 60 days from the
12 date of the county’s notice to the municipal corporation to enact an ordinance authorizing
13 the municipal corporation instead of the county to use a speed monitoring system at that
14 location.

15 2. A. WITHIN 120 DAYS AFTER RECEIPT OF AN
16 APPLICATION SUBMITTED BY A PROGRAM ADMINISTRATOR OR THE
17 PROGRAM ADMINISTRATOR’S CONTRACTOR FOR THE USE OF A SPEED
18 MONITORING SYSTEM UNDER THIS SUBPARAGRAPH, THE STATE HIGHWAY
19 ADMINISTRATION SHALL APPROVE OR DENY THE APPLICATION OR RETURN THE
20 APPLICATION AS INCOMPLETE.

21 B. IF THE ADMINISTRATION DOES NOT APPROVE, DENY,
22 OR RETURN AN APPLICATION IN ACCORDANCE WITH SUBSUBSUBPARAGRAPH A OF
23 THIS SUBSUBPARAGRAPH, THE APPLICATION SHALL BE DEEMED APPROVED.

24 C. A SPEED MONITORING SYSTEM CONTRACTOR
25 THAT SUBMITS AN APPLICATION UNDER THIS SUBPARAGRAPH SHALL CONSULT
26 WITH THE PROGRAM ADMINISTRATOR BEFORE AND AFTER THE SUBMISSION OF
27 THE APPLICATION.

28 D. A PROGRAM ADMINISTRATOR OR THE PROGRAM
29 ADMINISTRATOR’S CONTRACTOR MAY NOT SUBMIT MORE THAN 10
30 APPLICATIONS FOR THE USE OF A SPEED MONITORING SYSTEM UNDER THIS
31 SUBPARAGRAPH DURING ANY 30-DAY PERIOD.

32 SECTION 2. AND BE IT FURTHER ENACTED, That this Act shall take effect
33 October 1, 2026.
