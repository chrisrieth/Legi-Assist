AMENDMENT NO. 2
On page 3, after line 31, insert:

“8–2A–02.

(A) THIS SUBTITLE APPLIES ONLY TO A LANDLORD THAT MANAGES OR
OWNS FIVE OR MORE RESIDENTIAL RENTAL UNITS IN THE STATE, INCLUDING ANY
RESIDENTIAL RENTAL UNITS THAT THE LANDLORD OWNS OR CONTROLS:

(1) IN WHOLE OR IN PART;

(2) DIRECTLY OR INDIRECTLY; OR

(3) THROUGH ONE OR MORE LEGAL ENTITIES.

(B) THIS SUBTITLE DOES NOT APPLY TO OWNER–OCCUPIED
RESIDENTIAL RENTAL UNITS.”.
