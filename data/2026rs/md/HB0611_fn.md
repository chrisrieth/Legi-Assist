Prince George’s County – Alcoholic Beverages – Fees PG 317–26 
 
 
This bill increases the annual fees for various alcoholic beverages licenses and permits in 
Prince George’s County. In addition, the bill increases (1) application fees from $300 to 
$400, and (2) license transfer fees from $500 to $550. The bill takes effect July 1, 2026. 
 
 
Fiscal Summary 
 
State Effect:  The bill does not materially affect State operations or finances. 
 
Local Effect:  Prince George’s County annual license fee revenues increase by 
approximately 
$141,000 
beginning 
in 
FY 
2027, 
as 
discussed 
below. 
Prince George’s County expenditures are not affected. 
 
Small Business Effect:  Minimal. 
 
 
Analysis 
 
Bill Summary/Current Law:  The Prince George's County Board of License 
Commissioners is authorized to charge an annual fee for alcoholic beverages licenses and 
permits in the county. The bill increases most of these fees by varying amounts, as shown 
in Exhibit 1. The bill also increases the annual fee for a specified Class B beer, wine, and 
liquor license in the City of Hyattsville from $1,500 to $1,650. 
 
Local Revenues:  Based on the current number of alcoholic beverages licenses issued in 
Prince George’s County, the county estimates that license fee revenues will increase by 
approximately $141,000, beginning in fiscal 2027.
